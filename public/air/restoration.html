The Stack

An AI dispatch layer that replaces your answering service. Captures every emergency call, triages damage category per IICRC standards, and dispatches your crew — with the homeowner still on the line.

AIR-01

Emergency Triage

AI answers within seconds and immediately guides the homeowner — water shutoff location, electrical safety, salvageable items. Buys time while dispatching your crew.

AIR-02

Damage Classification

Captures damage type, water category (1/2/3), affected square footage, materials impacted, insurance carrier, policy status, and time since incident. Your crew arrives with a scope in hand.

Category 2+ water and all fire/smoke events trigger immediate SMS + call to your on-call crew. Non-emergency mold assessments get scheduled. Insurance documentation starts before your truck rolls.
