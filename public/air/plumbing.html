Section 02

The Stack

An AI dispatch layer that answers every emergency call, guides the homeowner through immediate damage control (shutoff, electrical safety), and dispatches your plumber — while the water is still being contained.

AIR-01

Emergency Guidance

AI answers within seconds and walks the homeowner through shutoff valve location, electrical hazard avoidance, and damage containment. Stops the bleeding before your truck rolls.

AIR-02

Problem Qualification

Captures pipe location, break type, water volume, affected rooms, shutoff status, water heater involvement, home age, and foundation type. Your plumber arrives knowing the scope.

AIR-03

Smart Dispatch

Active leaks and burst pipes trigger immediate SMS + call to your on-call plumber. Slow drains, fixture installs, and water heater quotes get scheduled. Morning inbox is triaged by severity.
