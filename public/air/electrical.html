Section 02

The Stack

An AI dispatch layer that answers every after-hours call, triages the emergency level (nuisance trip vs. fire risk), and routes dispatches to your on-call tech — while guiding the homeowner on immediate safety.

AIR-01

Safety-First Triage

AI answers within seconds and provides immediate safety guidance — main breaker location, what to do about burning smells, when to call 911 vs. wait. Buys time while dispatching your tech.

AIR-02

Problem Qualification

Captures problem type (outage, sparking, panel issue, rewire need), building age, panel type and age, number of affected circuits, symptoms, and urgency level. Your tech arrives informed.

AIR-03

Smart Dispatch

Fire risk and safety concerns trigger immediate SMS + call to your on-call tech. Non-emergency rewire estimates and fixture installs get scheduled into your calendar. Morning inbox is organized by priority.
