Section 02

The Stack

An AI layer that sits on top of your phone system. Captures every after-hours call, triages the emergency level, and routes dispatches to your on-call tech — before the customer calls someone else.

AIR-01

Emergency Triage

AI answers every call within seconds. Identifies the problem, assesses urgency (stuck open = security risk, broken spring = morning-safe), and routes accordingly.

AIR-02

Problem Qualification

Captures door type, opener brand, symptom description, age, warranty status, and whether the customer needs same-day or can wait. Separates emergencies from scheduled repairs.

AIR-03

Smart Dispatch

Emergencies trigger an immediate SMS to your on-call tech with address and problem details. Non-urgent requests get scheduled into your next available slot. Morning inbox is a stack of qualified jobs, not cold voicemails.
